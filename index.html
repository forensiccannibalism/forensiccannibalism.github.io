Welcome, cannibal!

Welcome to Forensic Cannibalism. Expect useless software, schizo-rants, and entire articles complaining about stupid things I see online. Forensic cannibalism is the act of metaphorically defiling the grave of an idea in order to satisfy a morbid craving for obsolete comfort. This is a prime example of forensic cannibalism. Someone born years after the days of Y2K larping as an early-2000's kid, and in the process, soiling the idea of what that era even looked like.

This site was named the way it was since it could ironically be considered forensic cannibalism against old websites. Though, I really just like making simple sites without animations and requests to steal your info flooding your screen.
